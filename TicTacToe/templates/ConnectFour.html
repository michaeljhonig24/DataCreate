Difficulty Information

Random (Easy)

The AI selects moves randomly from the available columns. This provides a low level of challenge and is suitable for beginners.

MiniMax (Medium)

The AI uses the Minimax algorithm with alpha-beta pruning to evaluate possible moves and select the optimal one. This creates a moderate challenge for players.

MCTS (Hard)

The AI employs Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) to explore potential future game states and make highly strategic decisions. This difficulty level is designed for experienced players.
